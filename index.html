The Ancient Outskirts

Follow Knit and Ytric into unknown lands against unforeseen threats while they learn to share a single consciousness.
A third person, melee combat, action-adventure level.

Level Design work:

  • Built level blockout, designed 2D map, planned combat spaces and encounters, designed puzzles.
  • Conceptualized and scripted narrative moments.
  • Main objectives: Introduce the player to Knit and Ytric's story and core gameplay mechanics. Hook the player with large-scale, exciting revelations.

Additional tasks:

  • Scripted melee combat system, enemy AI behaviors, interactive puzzle elements, custom gameplay mechanics.

Info:

  • Solo Project
  • Unreal Engine 4 (used Blueprints for scripting)
  • Maya (used for entire blockout)

About Me

Hi! I'm Austin.
I am drawn to level design because it perfectly combines the types of thinking and problem solving that I love - thinking logically, thinking creatively, and empathizing with others. I value creating games that engage players with compelling challenges and carry an emotional impact.

My B.S. degree is in Data Analytics, which provided me a strong background in computer science that I use to quickly prototype a variety of mechanics for my levels.
It also provided me with an understanding of how to listen deeply, take in information, consider it with purpose, and draw actionable conclusions. This is a vital part of my design process: listening to my own thoughts while designing, the feedback of others, and the needs of the game.

Everything I have learned while pursuing level design on my own has culminated in the portfolio above. These projects are those I am the most proud of and those that contributed the most to my progression (...thanks to a lot of mistakes made).
