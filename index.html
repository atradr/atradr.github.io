The Ancient Outskirts

Follow Knit and Ytric into unknown lands against unforeseen threats while they learn to share a single consciousness.
A third person, melee combat, action-adventure level.

My main objectives:

  • Gameplay goals: show the player the types of experiences they can expect the game to offer, teach core mechanics with organic tutorials, match Ytric's gameplay abilities with his narrative progression.
  • Narrative goals: hook the player with large-scale and exciting moments, establish Knit and Ytric's relationship, begin Ytric's growth in power and hint at his future potential.
